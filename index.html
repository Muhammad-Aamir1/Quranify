Daily Screen Lock
NurAl can show a verse on your screen each morning and keep it active until you've read it aloud. This is completely optional and you can turn it off anytime.
🔏
What this does
Shows a verse overlay on your first phone use each day. The overlay closes once you finish reading aloud.
🚫
What it does NOT do
Does not access messages, contacts, camera, or any personal data. Cannot prevent emergency calls.
⚙️
You stay in control
Disable this anytime in Settings → Screen Lock. One weekly skip is always available.
I understand and consent to the daily screen lock feature
📅
Your Weekly Skip
Life happens. Once per week, you can skip the daily reading without breaking your streak or triggering the lock.
✅
1 skip per rolling 7 days
Your skip resets exactly 7 days after you last used it — not on a fixed Sunday/Monday boundary.
🔥
Streak is protected
Using a skip marks the day as “excused” — your streak does not reset. Skipped days show in amber on your heatmap.
⚠️
Missing days without a skip
If you miss a day without using your skip, your streak resets to zero. The screen lock will still activate the next morning.

Enable Microphone Permission
On Chrome Mobile:
1. Tap the lock icon (🔒) in the address bar.
2. Select "Site settings" > "Permissions".
3. Set "Microphone" to "Allow".
4. Refresh the page and try again.

On Other Browsers:
Go to browser settings > Site permissions > Microphone > Allow for this site.

If using HTTP (not HTTPS), microphone may not work. Use a secure connection if possible.
